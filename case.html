Henry Dorset "Case"

Click for links Case might find interesting

         Case is Neuromancer's protaganist and antihero. Case is a Console Cowboy that can't go into the Cyberspace because of Nervous System damage caused by his former employer in retaliation for stealing from them. As a result, case is at the end of his rope in a suicidal and depressed state. He turns to dealing drugs and dragging his on-again off-again girlfriend Linda Lee into that world. After she is killed, Case offers his Cyber Cowboy skills to a man named Armitage because he promises to fix Case.

         While completeing the mission for the Wintermute AI, Case create an antihero bond with Molly and gets to see an AI creation of Linda, and changes dramatically over the course of the book. In retalition for the mental strife caused body the Neuromancer AI, he completes his mission for Wintermute, and joins the two together.

         At the end of the novel, Case decides to get off of drugs completely and make a new life for himself after developing a concept of self-worth. He "found a girl who called herself Michael," married her, and had four kids with her. He is never explicitly in William Gibson's Sprawl trilogy again.
