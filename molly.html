Molly "Millions"

Click for links Molly might find interesting

         Molly is another antihero in Neuromancer and Case's friend with benefits in the novel. She is a strong mercenary that appears to wear sunglasses all of the time because of her the cybernetic implants in her eyes. Molly appears very stoic and spiteful to most characters in the book, but is loyal and faithful as a companion of Case's. Some symbolism to contribute to the stoic, and strong force of nature she is: her eye implants route her tears to her mouth, so whenever she cries she will literally spit it out or swallow down her tears.

         Some other modifications to her person include, retractable razor fingers and much faster reflexes than any normal person.

         Throughout the course of the novel, Molly is Case's confidant and partner in their mission. the two trust each other and work with eachother to stay steps ahead of the AIs and Armitage their employer. Molly reveals to Case that her attraction to him is partly because he reminds her of her former lover Johnny Mnemonic.
