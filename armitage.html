Armitage

Click for links Armitage might find interesting

         Armitage is the man who recruits Molly and Case for the mission to unite Wintermute with Neuromancer. Armitage is actually a man named Willis Corto. Colonel Corto took part in a secret operation named Screaming Fist. The operation failed and he was heavily injured both physically and psychologically. Through Wintermute's "computer-mediated psychotherapy" Corto takes on the contructed personality of Armitage.

         Soon enough though, Armitage's personality slowly disintegrates. He begins to believe that Case is his subordinate during Screaming Fist operation, and attempts to escape imagined Russian forces using the escape pod. He is killed when he ejects into space with the airlock still open.
